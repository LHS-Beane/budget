Pay Cadence Notes

Biweekly pay is converted using 26 pay periods per year. That means this tool naturally captures the extra “two 3-paycheck months” effect instead of understating your annual income.
If a stipend is seasonal or not guaranteed, either lower the amount or put it in a separate income line so your core budget stays honest.

What this forecast means

This forecast is meant to answer questions like: “If we choose the Tesla option, what happens to our emergency fund, vacation fund, and debt momentum over 24 months?”
Forecasts are not promises. They are only as honest as the numbers you put in, especially for irregular spending and seasonal stipends.
